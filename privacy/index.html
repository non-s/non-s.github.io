Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ site.time | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

1. Information We Collect

GlobalBR News is a static website. We do not collect personal information directly. We may use third-party services that collect anonymous usage data.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics may set cookies on your device. The data we receive is anonymous and aggregated; we do not link it to any personal identifiers. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage to remember your preferences (theme, font size, reading list, dismissed banners). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Links

Our articles link to third-party news sources. We are not responsible for their privacy practices — please review the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

5. Content

GlobalBR News aggregates publicly available news. All original content rights belong to their respective publishers.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. As a static site we hold very little data, but if you have questions please contact us.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, see our Contact page or email contact@globalbrnews.com.
