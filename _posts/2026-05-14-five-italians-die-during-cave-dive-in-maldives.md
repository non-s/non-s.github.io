Five Italian divers died in a cave dive accident in the Maldives. The incident occurred at a depth of 50m, according to Italian authorities. Maldivian rescuers have so far recovered one body. The cause of the accident is not yet known. ## The accident is being investigated by local authorities. The Maldivian government has offered its condolences to the families of the victims. The [Maldives](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maldives) is a popular destination for scuba diving and snorkeling. ## The Italian government has confirmed the deaths of its citizens. The [Italian government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Italy) is working with the Maldivian authorities to investigate the accident. The incident has raised concerns about the safety of cave diving in the Maldives. The Maldivian government has promised to take measures to prevent such accidents in the future.
