SEOUL — South Korea has opened the Galaxy Robot Park in Seoul, a robot-themed entertainment venue designed to blend cutting-edge technology with family-friendly attractions. The park, located in the city’s Gangnam district, features humanoid robot boxing matches, K-pop dance performances and interactive AI exhibits tailored for children and adults alike.

The Galaxy Robot Park is the latest project by [Samsung](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsung), aimed at showcasing the company’s advancements in robotics and artificial intelligence. Visitors can watch live demonstrations of humanoid robots in simulated boxing matches, where robots equipped with sensors and AI algorithms compete in choreographed bouts. The performances highlight the precision and adaptability of modern robotics technology.

In addition to robot combat shows, the park hosts daily K-pop dance performances featuring holographic projections and robot dancers synchronized with popular K-pop hits. The entertainment lineup includes interactive AI attractions where children can engage in voice-controlled games, robot storytelling sessions and educational activities designed to teach programming and robotics basics.

## Seoul’s new tech entertainment hub
The Galaxy Robot Park spans over 2,000 square meters and is divided into themed zones, including a robot arena, a K-pop stage and an AI discovery area. Each zone offers hands-on experiences, such as operating robotic arms, programming simple AI tasks and participating in robot-led quiz games. The park’s design emphasizes accessibility, with attractions suitable for all age groups.

Officials from [Seoul Metropolitan Government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seoul) have hailed the park as a major step in promoting STEM education and fostering public interest in robotics. The project aligns with South Korea’s broader push to become a global leader in AI and robotics by 2030. The government has also announced plans to subsidize school field trips to the park to encourage youth engagement with technology.

## What’s inside the Galaxy Robot Park
Key highlights include the Robot Arena, where visitors can watch humanoid robots engage in fast-paced boxing matches powered by AI decision-making. The K-Pop Stage features holographic dancers and robotic performers backed by live and recorded K-pop tracks, creating a futuristic concert experience. The AI Discovery Zone offers workshops where families can learn about machine learning through interactive games and challenges.

Admission to the Galaxy Robot Park is open daily, with ticket prices ranging from $15 to $30 depending on the package selected. The park also offers VIP tours that include meet-and-greets with robot performers and exclusive access to behind-the-scenes technology demonstrations. Special discounts are available for students and senior citizens.

The opening of the Galaxy Robot Park reflects South Korea’s ambition to merge entertainment with technological innovation. By offering a mix of education and fun, the venue aims to inspire the next generation of engineers, programmers and tech enthusiasts while providing a unique experience for visitors from around the world.
