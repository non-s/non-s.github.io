Google is finally rolling out its long-rumored smart glasses this year, and it’s not just another tech demo. Sources familiar with the project say the hardware will blend AI assistance with practical tools like real-time language translation, putting it in direct competition with Meta’s [Ray-Ban Meta glasses](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ray-Ban_Meta). Unlike Google’s previous Glass Enterprise Edition—aimed at businesses—the new consumer version is designed for everyday use, with a sleeker design and lower price tag. Expect an announcement before the holiday shopping season hits full swing, likely in October or November.
