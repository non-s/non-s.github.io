Welcome to **TechBR News** — a technology news portal that updates itself automatically.

<!--more-->

## How it works

A Python script runs every 6 hours via GitHub Actions and collects the latest articles from trusted RSS feeds including TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, Ars Technica, MIT Technology Review and more.

Each article is published as a post with title, summary, source credit and a link back to the original.

## What you'll find here

- **Technology** — Hardware, software and digital trends
- **AI** — Machine learning, LLMs and the future of artificial intelligence  
- **Gadgets** — Reviews, releases and the best devices on the market
- **Startups** — Venture capital, funding rounds and new businesses
- **Security** — Cybersecurity, privacy and data protection
- **Mobile** — Smartphones, apps and operating systems

## Fully automated

No manual writing. No ads. No clutter. Just curated tech news from the world's best sources, delivered automatically.

Follow via [RSS Feed](/feed.xml) to stay updated.
