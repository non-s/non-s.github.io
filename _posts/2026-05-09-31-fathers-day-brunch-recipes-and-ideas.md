## Classic Brunch Staples with a Twist

A green eggs and ham frittata brings playful color and familiar flavors to the table. The dish combines scrambled eggs with diced ham and fresh herbs, baked until golden. For something sweeter, the blueberry coffee cake offers a tender crumb and bursts of juicy berries. Both recipes rely on simple ingredients but deliver impressive results, making them ideal for home cooks.

Bon Appétit’s lineup includes variations on classics like pancakes, waffles, and quiches. A savory bacon and cheddar quiche provides a rich option, while fluffy buttermilk pancakes topped with maple syrup offer a lighter choice. The recipes cater to different tastes and dietary needs, from gluten-free to vegetarian options. This versatility ensures every family can find a dish to love.

## Quick and Impressive Dishes for Busy Mornings

For those short on time, quick recipes like avocado toast with a poached egg or a cheesy breakfast casserole save the day. The avocado toast recipe tops whole-grain bread with smashed avocado, a runny egg, and chili flakes for heat. The breakfast casserole layers hash browns, sausage, cheese, and eggs into a one-dish wonder that bakes while you relax.

Other recipes focus on bold flavors. A spicy chorizo and potato hash brings Southwestern heat to the plate, while a lemon-ricotta pancake offers a bright, citrusy bite. These dishes prove that brunch doesn’t have to be complicated to taste extraordinary. Bon Appétit’s collection ensures there’s something for every skill level and palate.

## Make-Ahead Options for Stress-Free Brunching

Planning ahead can simplify Father’s Day brunch. Recipes like overnight French toast casserole or a breakfast strudel can be prepped the night before. The French toast casserole bakes in the morning with minimal effort, while the strudel delivers a flaky, layered pastry filled with apples or berries. Both options let hosts enjoy the celebration instead of slaving over the stove.

For those who prefer savory over sweet, a make-ahead breakfast burrito bar allows guests to customize their plates. Offer fillings like scrambled eggs, black beans, cheese, and salsa, then let everyone assemble their own burritos. This interactive approach turns brunch into a fun, engaging experience. Bon Appétit’s recipes emphasize convenience without sacrificing flavor.

Father’s Day brunch isn’t just about the food—it’s about the effortless joy of sharing a meal. Whether you opt for a showstopping centerpiece or a collection of small plates, the recipes from Bon Appétit provide inspiration for every type of celebration. Next year, consider expanding the menu with international flavors or themed brunch ideas to keep the tradition fresh.
