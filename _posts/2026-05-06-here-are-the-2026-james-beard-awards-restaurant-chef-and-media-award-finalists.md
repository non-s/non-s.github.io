The James Beard Foundation [James_Beard_Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Beard_Foundation) has released the full list of nominees for the 2026 James Beard Awards, marking one of the most anticipated events in the U.S. restaurant industry. The foundation announced the finalists across multiple categories, including Outstanding Restaurateur, Best New Restaurant, and Best Chef awards in each of its 12 regions. Media awards, such as Journalism and Broadcast Media, were also included in the announcement.

In February, the foundation revealed the winners of its America’s Classic Award, which honors six independently owned restaurants with enduring appeal. The Achievement Awards, recognizing industry leaders, were announced on March 31. This year’s nominees span a wide range of culinary styles and regions, reflecting the diversity of the American dining scene.

## Regional and National Categories Highlight Top Talent
The 2026 nominees represent chefs and restaurants from across the country, with categories divided into 12 regions. Best Chef awards are given in each region, while national categories include Outstanding Restaurant and Outstanding Chef. The foundation also recognizes Best New Restaurant, Outstanding Pastry Chef, and Outstanding Wine and Beverage programs. Media awards honor excellence in food journalism, including reporting, criticism, and visual storytelling.

No Us Without You, a nonprofit supporting immigrant and minority-owned restaurants, received special recognition as part of the Achievement Awards. Honorees Damián Diaz and Othon Nolasco were acknowledged for their work in the industry. The foundation’s awards are widely regarded as one of the highest honors in the restaurant world, often serving as a benchmark for culinary excellence.

## Media Awards Recognize Top Food Journalism
The 2026 James Beard Awards also include categories for food media, recognizing outstanding journalism in print, digital, and broadcast formats. Nominees in the Journalism category span investigative reporting, feature writing, and photography. Broadcast Media nominees highlight exceptional food-focused television and radio programs. The foundation’s media awards aim to elevate public discourse around food and dining.

Winners will be announced at a ceremony in June 2026, following a period of public voting and jury deliberation. The foundation has not yet disclosed the date or location of the event, but past ceremonies have been held in Chicago and New York. The awards ceremony is a major event in the food industry, drawing top chefs, restaurateurs, and media figures from across the country.

The 2026 James Beard Awards finalists reflect the evolving tastes and trends in the American dining scene. From fine dining to casual eateries, the nominees represent a broad spectrum of culinary innovation. Industry observers will closely watch the June ceremony to see which chefs and restaurants take home the prestigious awards.
