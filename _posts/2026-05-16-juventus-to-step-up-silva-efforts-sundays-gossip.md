Serie A champions Juventus have moved ahead of rivals in the race to sign Manchester City playmaker Bernardo Silva before Sunday’s transfer deadline. The 29-year-old Portuguese international has been a consistent starter for Pep Guardiola’s side but is now a priority for Juventus as they look to rebuild their midfield. Reports suggest the Old Lady is prepared to meet City’s valuation, which is believed to be around £60 million. Silva’s contract runs until 2025, but he’s never been a fan favorite at the Etihad Stadium, making a move more likely.
