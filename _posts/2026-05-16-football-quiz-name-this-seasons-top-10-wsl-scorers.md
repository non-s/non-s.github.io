The Women’s Super League (WSL) season concludes this weekend as teams battle for trophies and survival. With the final fixtures set for Saturday, goal-scoring charts have been reshaped in the league’s most competitive campaign yet.

## Women’s Super League’s top scorers ahead of final weekend

England striker [Beth Mead](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beth_Mead) of [Arsenal Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arsenal_Women_F.C.) tops the scoring list with 11 goals. Mead, the reigning Women’s Player of the Year, has been a consistent threat in front of goal, helping [Arsenal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arsenal_Women_F.C.) remain in contention for the title. Her performances have drawn comparisons to some of the WSL’s all-time greats, underlining her impact on this season’s competition.

[Chelsea Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chelsea_Women_F.C.) also have multiple representatives in the top 10, reflecting their dominance in the league. [Sam Kerr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Kerr), the Australian international, has scored 10 goals, while [Fran Kirby](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fran_Kirby) has contributed eight. Both players have been instrumental in [Chelsea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chelsea_Women_F.C.)’s push for the title, with Kerr’s physical presence and Kirby’s creativity forming a potent partnership.

## Who else features in the top 10?

[Rachel Daly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rachel_Daly) of [Aston Villa Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aston_Villa_Women_F.C.) sits third with nine goals, her pace and direct running causing problems for opposing defenses. [Birmingham City Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Birmingham_City_Women_F.C.) striker [Lorna Burnell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lorna_Burnell) has scored eight, while [Manchester United’s](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manchester_United_Women_F.C.) [Ellen White](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellen_White) rounds out the top five with seven goals.

Arsenal and Chelsea continue to set the pace in the scoring charts, but other teams have also made their mark. [Manchester City Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manchester_City_Women_F.C.)’s [Lauren Hemp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lauren_Hemp) has contributed six goals, while [Tottenham Hotspur Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tottenham_Hotspur_Women_F.C.)’s [Bethany England](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bethany_England) has five. [Everton Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everton_Women_F.C.)’s [Bethany Balcer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bethany_Balcer) and [Brighton & Hove Albion Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brighton_%26_Hove_Albion_Women_F.C.)’s [Kayleigh Green](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kayleigh_Green) each have four goals in the top 10.

The final weekend promises drama as teams fight to avoid relegation or secure a top-four finish. [Leicester City Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leicester_City_Women_F.C.), currently bottom of the table, face a must-win scenario to stand any chance of survival. Meanwhile, [West Ham United Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Ham_United_Women_F.C.) and [Reading Women](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reading_F.C._Women) are locked in a tight battle at the bottom, with both clubs needing points to avoid dropping into the Championship.

Title races remain tight, too. [Arsenal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arsenal_Women_F.C.) and [Chelsea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chelsea_Women_F.C.) are separated by just two points, with [Manchester City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manchester_City_Women_F.C.) also in the mix. The final day will decide whether an historic title goes to North London or if Chelsea can hold off their rivals for another year.

O futebol feminino britânico está prestes a viver um desfecho histórico no fim de semana, com a definição não só do campeão da Women’s Super League (WSL), mas também da artilheira da competição. Com a temporada chegando à sua reta final, as atenções se voltam para as dez maiores goleadoras do torneio, cujas performances podem redefinir o protagonismo das atletas em um esporte que ganha cada vez mais espaço na mídia e nas torcidas ao redor do mundo.

Para o público brasileiro, acostumado com o protagonismo da Seleção Feminina de Marta, Cristiane e Formiga em Copas do Mundo, a WSL representa um dos campeonatos mais competitivos do planeta, atraindo estrelas internacionais e revelando novos talentos. A presença de jogadoras como Sam Kerr, Beth Mead e Vivianne Miedema entre as principais artilheiras não só reforça a qualidade técnica da liga, mas também serve como inspiração para as meninas brasileiras que sonham em brilhar em solo europeu. Além disso, o desempenho dessas atletas pode influenciar diretamente na convocação da Seleção Brasileira, que busca renovar seu elenco para as próximas edições da Copa do Mundo e dos Jogos Olímpicos.

O grande destaque do fim de semana será a disputa acirrada pelo título de artilheira, que promete deixar a última rodada ainda mais eletrizante, enquanto o Brasil observa de perto o potencial de suas conterrâneas no exterior.
