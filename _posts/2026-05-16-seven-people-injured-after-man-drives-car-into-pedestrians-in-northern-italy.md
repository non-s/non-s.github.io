A driver in his 30s intentionally rammed a car into a crowd of pedestrians in [Modena](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modena), northern Italy, on Saturday, injuring seven people, police confirmed. The attack left two victims in serious condition, with injuries requiring hospitalization. Authorities took the suspect into custody within minutes of the incident after witnesses intervened and detained him until police arrived.

Investigators said the man, whose identity has not been released, allegedly attempted to stab a passerby who tried to stop him immediately after the collision. The bystander suffered minor injuries and was treated at the scene. Police did not disclose a motive for the attack but said they were reviewing surveillance footage and interviewing witnesses to piece together the events leading up to the crash.

## Police confirm suspect’s arrest, investigation underway
The driver, a local resident, was taken to a nearby police station for questioning. Authorities did not reveal if he made any statements regarding the incident. Prosecutors in Modena have opened a criminal case, focusing on charges of attempted murder and aggravated assault. The car involved in the attack was seized for forensic analysis as part of the ongoing probe.

Neighborhood residents described the area as a busy pedestrian zone near the city center, where locals often gather for shopping and socializing. The attack unfolded around midday on a busy Saturday, when foot traffic was at its peak. Police increased patrols in the area following the incident and urged residents to report any suspicious activity.

## Italy grapples with rising road violence incidents
The attack in Modena adds to a recent surge in road-related violence in Italy, where traffic accidents and deliberate ramming incidents have drawn public concern. Authorities in several cities have implemented stricter traffic controls and surveillance in response. The government has also called for public awareness campaigns to address road safety and mental health issues linked to such incidents.

Local officials expressed shock over the attack, calling it an isolated but disturbing event. The mayor of Modena, [Gian Carlo Muzzarelli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gian_Carlo_Muzzarelli), issued a statement extending support to the victims and their families while condemning the violence. Investigators continue to explore all possible motives, including mental health factors, though no official conclusions have been reached.

Authorities have not released the names of the injured or further details about their conditions. The investigation is ongoing, and police have requested public assistance in providing additional information that could aid the case.
