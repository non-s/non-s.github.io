A fresh take on the 1980s Choose Your Own Adventure series debuts this summer with *Choose Your Own Adventure: Space and Beyond*, a graphic novel that invites readers to navigate the solar system through interactive storytelling.

The book, set for release in July 2025, reimagines the original 1984 text-based adventure with vibrant illustrations and branching narratives. Readers choose their path through space missions, alien encounters, and planetary discoveries, blending nostalgia with modern comic artistry.

Publisher [Workman Publishing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Workman_Publishing) collaborated with the original series’ creators to ensure the new edition stays true to the franchise’s spirit while appealing to today’s graphic novel audience. The adaptation includes updated science facts and expanded storylines not present in the 1980s version.

Illustrator [Dan Dougherty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_Dougherty) leads the visual direction, known for his work on *Beowulf: The Graphic Novel*. His art style merges retro sci-fi aesthetics with contemporary comic techniques, creating a fresh yet familiar look for the series.

## Interactive science meets classic storytelling
The graphic novel retains the original’s hallmark branching narratives, where readers’ choices determine the story’s outcome. Each path explores different celestial bodies, from Mars to Jupiter’s moons, with scientifically plausible scenarios. NASA-inspired concepts guide the adventure, ensuring accuracy in planetary descriptions and space travel mechanics.

Early previews highlight standout sequences, including a mission to Saturn’s rings and a close encounter with Europa’s icy surface. The book also features behind-the-scenes notes on how real space science influenced the storylines, adding an educational layer for young readers.

## Why this adaptation stands out
Unlike standard graphic novels, *Space and Beyond* retains the interactivity of the original books, allowing readers to flip to different pages based on their choices. Workman Publishing plans a digital companion app to enhance the experience, offering animated illustrations and sound effects for select scenes.

The project aligns with a broader trend of reviving classic children’s series with modern formats. Recent successes like *Choose Your Own Adventure* video games and podcasts prove the franchise’s enduring appeal, particularly among Gen Z and millennial parents seeking nostalgic content for their kids.

With print and ebook editions available at launch, the graphic novel targets readers aged 8 to 12 but promises appeal for all ages. Pre-order bonuses include a collectible poster and a QR code linking to exclusive space science videos.

Workman Publishing will host a virtual launch event in June 2025 featuring Dougherty and a NASA scientist discussing the science behind the story.
