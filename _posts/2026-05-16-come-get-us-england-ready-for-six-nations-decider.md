Borthwick’s side have already secured the Triple Crown for the second year running, so the Six Nations trophy is the only prize left to play for. France’s [Antoine Dupont](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antoine_Dupont), one of world rugby’s best players, will be desperate to add to his collection. But England’s [Owen Farrell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Owen_Farrell) is the man with the most to prove. England are the experienced big dogs, but France are the hungry underdogs with nothing to lose. The weather’s also a factor.
