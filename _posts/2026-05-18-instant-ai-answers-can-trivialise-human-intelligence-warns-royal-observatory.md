The [Royal Observatory Greenwich](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Observatory_Greenwich), the historic site that shaped modern astronomy, is raising a red flag about artificial intelligence. Paddy Rodgers, who ran the observatory for nearly a decade, says the problem isn’t AI itself—it’s how we use its answers without thinking them through. "We’re trading depth for speed," he told the BBC. "That’s a trade we might regret."
