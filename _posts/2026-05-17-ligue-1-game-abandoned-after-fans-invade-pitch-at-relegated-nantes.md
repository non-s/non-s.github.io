The French league confirmed the match would not be replayed, meaning Toulouse left with the two points in their pocket. The incident happened at Stade de la Beaujoire, Nantes' home ground, around 20 minutes into the first half on Saturday afternoon. Video footage showed several hooded figures sprinting onto the pitch from behind one goal, carrying flares that they immediately ignited. Thick orange smoke billowed across the pitch, forcing players and staff to cover their faces and retreat toward the dressing rooms. Referee Clément Turpin blew his whistle to halt the game before it could continue, and the stadium announcer quickly urged fans to leave the stands for their safety. By the time order was restored, the damage was done. The French Football Federation (FFF) released a statement condemning the disruption and confirming the abandonment. They also announced an investigation would begin immediately, targeting the individuals responsible for the pitch invasion.
