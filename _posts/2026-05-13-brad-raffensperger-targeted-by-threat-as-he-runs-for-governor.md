The motive behind the threat was not publicly known, but it has raised concerns over the safety of political candidates in the US. ## The Incident and Its Aftermath: The discovery of a suspicious object found inside a vending machine at a campaign stop in Macon prompted the campaign to move the event outside. The incidents are an apparent reminder of the potent threat of political violence in the US, and the need for increased security measures to protect candidates and their supporters. ## Implications and Concerns: The threat against Raffensperger has sparked concerns over the rising tide of political violence in the US.
